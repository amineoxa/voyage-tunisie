ITINERARY PREVIEW

Breathtaking TUNISIA

5 Days of Ancient Ruins, Deserts & Mediterranean Dreams

  • Day 1: Tunis
  • Day 2: El Jem
  • Day 3: Sahara
  • Day 4: Tozeur
  • Day 5: Medina
1
Monday • Mediterranean Coast

Tunis & The Blue Pearl

Sidi Bou Said & Carthage

Begin your journey in the iconic blue-and-white village of Sidi Bou Said. Wander through bougainvillea-covered alleys overlooking the Mediterranean. After lunch, explore the UNESCO Roman ruins of Ancient Carthage and the legendary Antonine Baths.

2
Tuesday • Roman Heritage

Colosseum & Cave Houses

El Jem Amphitheatre

Drive south to El Jem to witness Africa's largest Roman amphitheatre, a rival to Rome's Colosseum. Later, arrive in Matmata to explore unique troglodyte cave dwellings, an ancient Berber way of life that inspired sci-fi architecture.

3
Wednesday • The Sahara

The Gateway to the Desert

Douz Camel Trek

Arrive at Douz, the "Gateway to the Sahara". Trade your car for a camel and ride into the sweeping sand dunes as the sun sets. Enjoy an authentic Berber dinner under the stars at a luxury desert camp.

4
Thursday • Movie Magic

Salt Lakes & Space Sets

Chott El Jerid & Mos Espa

Cross the shimmering salt flats of Chott El Jerid, where mirages are frequent. Reach Tozeur and visit the Mos Espa film set used in Star Wars, a surreal village of domes preserved in the desert dunes.

5
Friday • Cultural Soul

Sacred Echoes & Farewell

Kairouan & Tunis Medina

Head back north via Kairouan, the fourth holiest city in Islam, to see the Great Mosque. End your journey back in Tunis with a vibrant walk through the UNESCO Medina, buying final souvenirs in the souks.
